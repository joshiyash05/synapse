About

The world is facing an unprecedented challenge with communities and economies everywhere affected by the growing COVID-19 pandemic.

Donations support community’s work, including with partners, to track and understand the spread of the virus; to ensure patients get the care they need and frontline workers get essential supplies and information; and to accelerate research and development of a vaccine and treatments for all who need them.

The outpouring of global solidarity and support sparked by this shared challenge has been phenomenal. Givers Club is also Starting Vaction center .

Alone we can do so little. Together, we can do so much. This pandamic too shall pass, and in the end, there’s a bright light waiting for all of us at the end of this dark tunnel.Individually, we are just one drop. TOGETHER, WE ARE AN OCEAN.
